Pizza's history can be traced back to ancient Mediterranean
civilizations, lwhere flatbreads were a common food.
The modern pizza originated in Naples, Italy in the late 1800s

Brief history of Pizza

  • The word "pizza" may come from the Greek word pitta, which means "pie".
  • The Romans loved flatbreads, often topped with cheese and herbs.
  • The first recorded mention of pizza was in Gaeta, Italy in 997 AD.

The history of donuts can be traced back to ancient civilizations,
but the modern donut originated in 19th century America.

Brief History of the Donut

  • Early civilizations, including the Greeks, Romans, and Chinese, enjoyed fried dough treats
  • The Bible mentions fried cakes mixed with oil as an acceptable offering to God
  • Dutch settlers brought recipes for "olykoeks" (oily cakes) to New Amsterdam, which later became New York City

Tacos originated in Mexico and are a combination
of ancient Mexican recipes and international influences.
The word "taco" may come from the Nahuatl word tlahco,
which means "half" or "in the middle".

Breif history of Tacos

  • Tacos date back to pre-Hispanic times
  • The Aztecs used tortillas as edible spoons filled with fish or roasted meats
  • The first tacos were made with fresh corn tortillas, fish, and organs
  • Tacos were a staple meal for ancient Mexicans
